How do I install the app on my iPhone? +

iPhone Installation (Safari only):

  1. Open Safari browser (not Chrome)
  2. Go to survivebackpacking.com
  3. Tap the Share button (square with arrow)
  4. Select "Add to Home Screen"
  5. Tap "Add" to confirm

The app icon will appear on your home screen instantly!

How do I add more than 3 debts in the calculator? +

Unlimited debt entries!

  1. Go to Budget tab ‚Üí Debt Payoff Calculator
  2. Click the green "Add Debt" button

The app isn't loading or showing errors +

Quick fixes to try:

  1. Refresh the page - swipe down or press F5
  2. Clear browser cache - Settings ‚Üí Clear browsing data
  3. Check internet connection - try other websites
  4. Update your browser - use latest version
  5. Try incognito/private mode - rules out extensions

Still not working? Email us at support@survivebackpacking.com with details!

My data disappeared or isn't syncing +

Don't panic! Your data is likely safe:

  1. Check offline status - look for sync indicator
  2. Connect to WiFi - data may be waiting to sync
  3. Refresh the app - force a sync attempt
  4. Check browser storage - data might be saved locally

Prevention: Your data auto-saves every change and syncs when online.
